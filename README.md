# NASA-CDSS-Framework

## Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) for Long-Duration Spaceflight

This repository serves as a collaborative space for exploring the design and development of an AI-enabled Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) for long-duration human spaceflight.

## Vision

Develop a modular, trustworthy, and human-centered CDSS architecture capable of supporting astronaut health monitoring, risk assessment, decision support, and mission planning during long-duration space missions.

## Initial Areas of Interest

### AI Governance

- Transparency and explainability
- Human oversight
- Risk management
- Trustworthy AI principles
